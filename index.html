About DECA

What is DECA?

DECA prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality, management, and business. At Northfield, members compete, practice professional communication, travel to conferences, and build skills they can use far beyond high school.

01

Roleplays

Roleplays are individual competitive events where competitors receive a business scenario, get a short prep time, and present a solution to a judge. Roleplay competitors also take an exam, so the final score includes both presentation skills and business knowledge.

02

Writtens

Written events are prepared before competition. Competitors create a business plan, campaign, or proposal, then present it to judges. Most of the work happens ahead of time through research, writing, design, and presentation practice.

03

Team Decision Making

Team Decision Making, or TDM, is basically a two-person roleplay. Partners prep together, build one solution, and present as a team. It is great for people who like collaboration and quick thinking.
