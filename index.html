About Us

What is DECA?

DECA is a student organization that helps high school students build skills in business, leadership, communication, problem-solving, and professional confidence.

Members compete in roleplays, written business plans, finance events, entrepreneurship events, marketing events, and more. DECA is also a great way to meet people, travel, strengthen your resume, and practice real-world skills.

Why Join?

  • Compete in business events
  • Practice public speaking
  • Build leadership experience
  • Meet new people
  • Prepare for college and careers
