About DECA

What is DECA?

DECA prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality, management, and business. At Northfield, members compete, practice professional communication, travel to conferences, and build skills they can use far beyond high school.

01

Roleplays

Roleplays are individual competitive events where you are given a business scenario and a short prep time before presenting your solution to a judge. These events also include a cluster exam, so your score combines both your presentation and business knowledge.

02

Writtens

Written events are longer projects where competitors create a business plan, campaign, or proposal before competition. Instead of solving a scenario on the spot, you prepare a paper and presentation ahead of time.

03

Team Decision Making

Team Decision Making, or TDM, is basically a roleplay with two people. Partners work together during prep time, organize a solution, and then present as a team to the judge.
